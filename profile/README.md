## KUNLING-AI · 鲲灵智算

> **Let AI handle intelligence, let humans return to value creation.**  
> **把 AI 的事交给 AI，让人回归人的价值。**

**Research Together · Create Together · Share Together**  
**共研 · 共创 · 共享**

KUNLING (**Knowledge-augmented Universal Neural Learning for Intelligent Numerical Geoscience**) is an AI-driven numerical geoscience framework developed by Dr. Ze Liu’s research group at Ocean University of China.

KUNLING 是由中国海洋大学刘泽博士研究组开发的知识增强型智能数值地学框架，致力于融合物理模型、地学知识与人工智能。

## Research Directions · 研究方向

- **Multi-Scale Modeling & Earth-System Coupling**  
  **多尺度数值建模与地球圈层耦合**

- **Intelligent Data Assimilation & Deep-Time Reconstruction**  
  **智能数据同化与深时重建**

- **Tectonic–Climate–Sedimentary Process Modeling**  
  **构造—气候—沉积过程模拟**

- **Paleogeomorphology & Paleoenvironment Inversion**  
  **古地貌与古环境智能反演**

- **Global Multimodal Geoscience Vector Database**  
  **全球多模态地学向量数据库**

We build efficient, trustworthy, and open AI tools for understanding Earth across scales and deep time.

我们致力于构建高效、可信、开放的地学智能工具，探索跨尺度、深时域的地球系统演化。
